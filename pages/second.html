About Engaged

Engaged in a web application that analyzes how much engagement a headline will get by calculating its engagement score on a scale of 100 based on three criteria: the length of the headline, its sentiment, and word choices.

The engagement score is not a perfect measure of how a headline will perform in real life; it is more of a visualization of the negativity bias in journalism, where people are more inclined to click on news that are more negative.
